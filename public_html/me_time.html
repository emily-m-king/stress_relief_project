Listening to Music

According to the University of Nevada Ren’s counseling services department, different speeds of music create different emotions. Faster music can make you feel alert and concentrate better. Upbeat music can make you feel more optimistic and a slower tempo can relax your muscles and make you feel soothed.

Getting a Pedi and Meni

When feeling overwhelmed, messages and looking good can boost someone’s mood and confidence. The near-hour experience can reduce the stress a person is experiencing due to the relaxed environment. Using products like eucalyptus and/ or peppermint Epsom salt can add to the stress relief.

Binge Hulu/Netflix

Sometimes you just need to sit and watch your favorite shows. You do not need to be doing physical activities. When you are watching a show or a movie, your mind is more focused on the plot of the story that you temporarily forget everything outside of the storyline.

Me Time

“Me Time” includes anything that you feel like doing when you feel stressed and overwhelmed. These small activities keep your mind active and not limit your options. Stress relief activities can be whatever you want it to be.
