Through Exercise


Walking

Daily or weekly timed walks are beneficial in assisting to relieve stress. Walking with your back straight and letting your arms naturally swing as you walk are some ways to ensure that you allow your body to distress. Over time, one can increase their distance of walking or work on increasing the speed of their walk.

Dancing

Dancing is a fun and expressive way to get some exercise. There are many forms of dance to try; from ballet to jazz to ballroom, each offers its own challenges and style.

Yoga

Yoga is a form of relaxation that helps to calm the body, mind, and control breathing. This form of exercising is peaceful when performed in a space that is free of distractions. You can choose to position yourself in any way that suits your comfort. Set a timer for each position and work through taking deep breaths in and out.

Lifting

Lifitng is a great strength-building form of exercise. There are many muscle groups you can work on improving through various lifting exercises. Make sure you are following good safety practices and be sure to consult an expert if you have any questions or concerns about your lifting routine.
